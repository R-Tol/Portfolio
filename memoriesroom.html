Memories Room

Memories Room is an innovative VR puzzle game that blends linguistic and object-based challenges. ​

Presented at Expo Milano 2016, the game invites players to work together in a shared virtual space to solve puzzles centered around assembling objects and matching syllables. ​

The gameplay emphasizes cooperation and communication as players explore the VR environment, discover clues, and piece together linguistic elements that unlock further stages.
