Voxel AR

This application leverages Kinect technology to create voxel-based avatars that mimic users' movements in real time. ​

Designed as an engaging AR experience, the app allows users to see themselves as pixelated, dynamic avatars that respond to their actions, creating a fun and immersive interaction. ​

This technology was showcased in a prominent electronics store, transforming their display window into an interactive attraction. ​

Customers could step in front of the screen to see their movements replicated by their voxel avatars, creating a memorable, tech-driven shopping experience that draws attention to the store’s cutting-edge offerings, like computers and electronic devices.
