Back To Earth

This intense, cooperative puzzle game puts players in the high-stakes environment of a malfunctioning spacecraft.

Inspired by the dynamics of Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes, the game requires teamwork and communication as one player navigates the ship's intricate control panels, while the others consult an instruction manual to provide guidance and solve complex problems.

Set within a futuristic spaceship, players must manage time-sensitive tasks, troubleshoot errors, and overcome escalating challenges to prevent catastrophic failure.

With its immersive space setting and engaging, interactive puzzles, Back To Earth offers a thrilling experience that emphasizes collaboration under pressure.
